Ethan & Emma

Ethan & Emma are…

.

They say "marry your best friend."

We couldn't agree more.

We’re the kind of couple who talk nonstop, laugh at things only we find funny, and genuinely love being around each other. We’re partners in the truest sense: authentic, loving, and effortlessly in sync. Even after six years, we’re still as captivated by each other as we were at sixteen.

As we count down the days to our marriage, our hope is that it keeps feeling like this. That we continue loving each other as we do now: wholeheartedly, easily, and with unending joy. As we continue to grow and change, we can't wait to fall in love with the new versions of each other, again and again.
