His Story

Ethan’s someone who genuinely loves connecting with people. Whether he’s meeting someone new or spending time with people he’s known forever, he’s in his element when he’s building real connections.

He grew up in Lake Zurich, Illinois (just like Emma) and is the oldest of two brothers. These days, he’s working toward his master’s in Higher Education Administration at George Mason University and serving as a Graduate Residential Coordinator on campus. He's excited to go into a career where he can make an impact on students' lives and college experiences.

Emma would describe Ethan as easygoing, thoughtful, and incredibly nostalgic. He loves Minecraft and Mario Kart, and he’s a total sucker for a throwback movie night. He played cello and sang in choir growing up, so music has always had a place in his life. While Ethan loves classical music, car rides with Emma have added a bit more pop to his playlists.

Ethan’s slowly introducing Emma to the movies he can’t believe she’s never seen (he’s found she’ll agree to watch as long as he makes popcorn). But more than anything, he loves spending quality time with her. Whether it’s a long walk, a drive, or a Minecraft session on the couch where they can catch up for hours, those moments together are what he cherishes most.
